# Echo Memory Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Echo Memory is an offline game published by DolfinMind. This policy applies to
the Android application with package name `com.dolfinmind.echomemory`.

## Data collection and sharing

Echo Memory does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app
does not contain advertising, analytics, tracking, account, social, or backend
SDKs. The production Android app does not request internet access or sensitive
device permissions.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores game scores, streaks, play statistics, tutorial completion,
daily-challenge status, and the haptic setting locally on the device. This data
is used only to provide app functionality and is never sent to DolfinMind or a
third party.

Local data remains until the user clears the app's storage or uninstalls the
app. Because DolfinMind never receives this data, it cannot retrieve or delete
it remotely.

## Children's privacy

Echo Memory is not specifically directed to children and does not knowingly
collect data from anyone, including children.

## Security

The app minimizes privacy risk by operating without a network connection and
without collecting personal or sensitive information. Device-level storage
security is provided by the Android operating system.

## Changes to this policy

If a future release adds online services, advertising, analytics, or new data
practices, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be
updated before that release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact DolfinMind at dolfinmind@gmail.com.
