## Full description

Echo Memory is a focused offline memory game built around one simple idea:
watch a visual pattern, hold it in mind, then echo it back.

Every round grows a little more challenging. Clear color-and-icon cues make
patterns easy to read, while streak multipliers and transparent round bonuses
reward accurate recall. Sessions are quick to start, easy to understand, and
free from distracting pop-ups.

EIGHT WAYS TO TEST YOUR RECALL

• Classic Echo — repeat a growing sequence at Beginner, Expert, or Master pace
• Daily Challenge — play one deterministic challenge each day
• Practice — learn without a timer or limited lives
• Lumina Matrix — remember illuminated positions on a growing grid
• Reflex Match — decide whether consecutive cards match
• Echo Stream — identify the missing item in a visual sequence
• Quantum Flux — repeat the pattern after every signal moves
• Zen Echo — relax into endless recall without a timer or lost lives

FAIR, READABLE SCORING

Earn points for every correct signal. Longer streaks raise the multiplier, and
round bonuses reflect sequence length, accuracy, difficulty, and remaining
time. The score shown on screen is the score you earned—no mystery boosts.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Echo Memory works completely offline. It has no account, no advertising, no
analytics, and no internet permission. High scores, settings, and tutorial
progress remain on your device.

Pick a mode, focus on the signal, and see how long you can keep the memory loop
going.
